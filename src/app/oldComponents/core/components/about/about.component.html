EMAUA

We are a company that have the main aim to help you to structure the knowledge in an easier way. This knowledge that is spread and is useful could be structure in a way that you have courses and subjects. If you want to give us any suggestion contact us on: suggestion@emaua.com

Courses

Is correspondent to a Course in the University, like Computer Science, Physics or Math. This course is made by many different Subjects.

Subjects

Is like the Subjects you have at the different courses. Computer Science have Calculus One and Law course can have Introduction to the law system. Some courses have common subjects, and emaua allow those courses to reuse those common subjects.

Lessons

According to Wikipedia: A lesson is a structured period of time where learning is intended to occur. One Subject can have one or more Lessons. For example: imagine that you have the subject Calculus One: you can structure it in an ordered way that it will have many other lessons: Introduction to Calculus One, Limits, Derivates, etc...

Lectures

Lectures consists on videos of each of the Lessons that are designed, those videos could be from youtube, vimeo or Daylimotion.
